Description

SIMBODIES Advanced Infant Manikins are life-like pediatric patient simulators, frequently used by medical educators and clinicians for training purposes. 
These manikins replicate the look and appearance of male or female infant patients, approx. 10 months old, & are made from skin-safe, prosthetic-grade platinum silicone.

MANIKIN DETAIL

  • Needle decompression site: 2nd intercostal space, midclavicular line (anterior)
  • Intraosseous (IO) access capability at both tibia and femur
  • Partially open eyes with manual eyelid movement
  • Splashproof and washable skin
  • Available with male or female genitalia
  • Hand-punched hair for added realism
  • Integrated Bluetooth speakers for enhanced training scenarios
