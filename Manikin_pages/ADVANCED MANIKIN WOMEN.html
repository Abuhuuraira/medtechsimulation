ADVANCED MANIKIN WOMEN

Description

Our Advanced Women Manikin is a purpose-built to improve medical confidence in life-saving environments and reduce hesitation when delivering life-saving trauma care.

This manikin facilitates the education and practice of advanced procedures like ventilation, needle decompression, CPR and more.
