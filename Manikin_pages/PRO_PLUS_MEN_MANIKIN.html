Description

The Man Pro Plus Manikin line is designed for professional level training. The Pro-Plus Manikins include all features and capabilities of Pro Manikins, with the addition of an Ultimate SIM-Sleeve Kit and an Instant Moulage Kit. Built to perform in demanding field environments, from the beach to the Arctic, these manikins are engineered to withstand the toughest training conditions.
