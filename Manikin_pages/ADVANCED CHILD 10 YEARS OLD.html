​ADVANCED CHILD 10 YEARS OLD

Description

The Advanced Child Male/Female Manikins are highly realistic pediatric patient simulators designed for use by medical educators and clinicians in training environments. 
These manikins accurately replicate the look and anatomy of male or female child patients approximately 10 years of age and are crafted from skin-safe, prosthetic-grade platinum silicone to ensure lifelike texture and durability.
