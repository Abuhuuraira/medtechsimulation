ADVANCED MANIKIN WOMEN

Our Advanced Woman Manikin is a purpose-built to improve clinical confidence in all training environments and reduce hesitation when delivering lifesaving trauma care. This manikin facilitates the education and practice of advanced procedures like ventilation, needle decompression, CPR and more.

Features and Benefits

  • Anatomically correct, environmentally rugged, reusable airway
  • Realistic procedures such as intubation and CPR
  • Durable, upper and lower extremity wounds
  • Displaceable humeral head, ribs & sternum
