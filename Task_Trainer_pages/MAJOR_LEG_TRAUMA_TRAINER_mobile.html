MAJOR LEG TRAUMA TRAINER

Description

Our Major Leg Trauma Trainer features large cavity wounds and integrated bleeding tubes to simulate catastrophic bleeding using a manual pump.
With its lifelike look, feel, and weight, our Major Leg Trauma Trainer allows for an immersive simulation of tourniquet application, as well as hemorrhage and gunshot wound management.
Whether used on its own or alongside a SIMBODIES manikin, the trainer provides immediate feedback on proper tourniquet use and bleeding control making it an essential tool for medics, first responders, military personnel, and clinical educators.
