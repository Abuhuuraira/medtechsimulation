EMS TORSO TRAINER

Description

The EMS Torso Trainer is a versatile simulation tool designed to support a wide range of life-saving medical procedures in a single, compact, anatomically accurate model.
Built for EMS professionals, educators, and trainees, it delivers high-fidelity realism for skill development in both classroom and field settings.
Crafted from lifelike materials with detailed anatomical landmarks, the torso allows practice of airway management, needle decompression, chest seal application, ECG lead placement, and more. This trainer helps users build competence and confidence in critical interventions.

MANIKIN DETAIL

  • Chest drain placement at 5th intercostal space, anterior axillary line
  • Intraosseous (IO) infusion capability at the humeral head
  • Airway capable of intubation and ventilation
  • Nasal passages compatible with nasopharyngeal airway
