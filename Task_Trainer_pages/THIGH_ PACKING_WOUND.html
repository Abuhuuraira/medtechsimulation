THIGH PACKING WOUND

Description

This full-size human thigh model features large internal cavities, a superficial deep oval abrasion, gunshot wounds with soft tissue, and bone equipped with integrated bleeding tubes.
The Thigh Wound Packing Trainer provides a high-fidelity, hands-on experience for practicing one of the most critical trauma interventions: wound packing.
Designed to simulate a deep, bleeding thigh injury, it mimics the feel and resistance of real tissue, enabling learners to develop proper technique and confidence under pressure.
Part of our advanced range of hemorrhage control task trainers, this model delivers lifelike bleeding simulations to challenge and prepare medics, educators, and first responders for real-world emergencies.
