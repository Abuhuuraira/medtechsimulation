BLASTED ADULT

Description

Our Lifeless Blasted Body Manikins are separated into 7–18 different parts are most commonly used to replicate bodies of bombing scenarios. Our Lifeless Manikins are variations of our original manikins, designed and hand made to replicate the blasted and burnt body parts of a deceased person. These manikins are ideal for specific training scenarios and feature no clinical capabilities.

MANIKIN DETAIL
